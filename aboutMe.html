Design and Living

My journey into design began with a lifelong fascination with creativity and visual expression. From walking in fashion shows and styling clothing to running a successful K-pop fan account with over 7k followers in middle school, I've always been drawn to using design as a medium for storytelling and connection. Though I initially pursued pre-med studies at UC Davis with dreams of becoming a surgeon, my creative passions remained a constant thread throughout my academic journey, leading me to explore graphics and animation courses even while focused on the sciences.

During my time at UC Davis, I began reflecting on my true calling and rediscovered my passion for design. I realized it offered the perfect intersection of creativity, problem-solving, and human-centered innovation that I was seeking. I pivoted my major to Design and immersed myself in visual storytelling, finding a renewed sense of purpose and direction. This journey has led me to pursue UX design, where I can combine my analytical background with my creative instincts to create meaningful digital experiences.
